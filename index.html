SCHURZ

INNOVATION

CHALLENGE

The Schurz Innovation Challenge is a competition for students who are interested in bringing their innovative ideas to life through an incubation style process. All that is needed to compete is an innovative idea!

What do I need to compete?

You don't have to have developers on your team to compete. All you need is a unique idea. If you are looking for developers, you can apply to have your app coded. For more information on that process, click below.
