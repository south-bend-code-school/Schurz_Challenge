CHALLENGE

BRING YOUR IDEA TO LIFE

The Schurz Innovation Challenge is a competition for students who are interested in bringing their innovative ideas to life through an incubation style process. All that is needed to compete is an innovative idea and the desire to bring that idea to life!

What Do You Need to Compete?

You don't have to have developers or business majors on your team to compete. All you need is a unique and innovative idea. If you are looking for developers or help with your business plan, we have you covered. For more information on the incubation process...
